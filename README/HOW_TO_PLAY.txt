CONTROLS:
	Player 1:
		Movement: Arrows key
		Primary Fire: ,
		Secondary Fire: .

	Player 2:
		Movement: WASD
		Primary Fire: v
		Secondary Fire: b

GAMEPLAY:
	Primary Fire -> Hold down button to charge your shot (becomes faster and travels farther)

	Secondary Fire -> pickup certain powerups on map in order to gain a secondary fire for a short time
